# Privacy Policy




**Effective Date:** February 20, 2025

Thank you for using AutoWallX. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

### Information We Do Not Collect

AutoWallX does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not require account creation, track user behavior, or process any personally identifiable information.

### Data Handling and Storage

AutoWallX only stores the file paths of two user-selected wallpapers on your local device. This data is used solely to switch wallpapers based on the selected theme and is never transmitted, shared, or accessed by us or any third parties.

### Third-Party Services

AutoWallX does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. No external entities have access to any user data.

### Security

Since AutoWallX does not collect or store any personal information, there is no risk of unauthorized access to user data. All settings and preferences remain entirely on your local device.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website: [surajc.com](https://themedmac.com/).
