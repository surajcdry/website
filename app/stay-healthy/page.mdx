# Stay Healthy




**Privacy Policy for Stay Healthy Chrome Extension**

Effective Date: Nov 11, 2024

Thank you for choosing "Stay Healthy," an extension designed to support healthy computing habits by sending reminders to take regular breaks. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines the information collected by Stay Healthy, its usage, and your privacy rights.

### 1\. Information We Collect

**Browser Open Time:**  
Stay Healthy tracks the time when your browser was opened to help calculate intervals for break notifications. No other personal or browsing data is collected.

### 2\. How We Use Your Information

**Break Reminders:**  
The browser open time is used solely to calculate 20-minute intervals for sending break notifications. This information is not shared, stored, or transmitted outside your device.

### 3\. Data Retention

All timing information is used temporarily within the session and is reset when the browser is closed. No data is retained after your browsing session ends.

### 4\. Security

Stay Healthy is designed with user privacy as a priority. The extension does not transmit data to external servers, and no identifiable information is collected, stored, or shared.

### 5\. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or retained, there are no user actions required for managing or deleting data.

### 6\. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect new functionalities or user feedback. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review the policy periodically.

### 7\. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at suraj@surajc.com.

By using the Stay Healthy extension, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
